# 🔒 Privacy Policy

**SpendArc** is built with a privacy-first philosophy. Your financial data belongs to you — and only you.

---

## Our Core Principles

1. **Your data stays on your device.**
   SpendArc does not upload, sync, or transmit your financial data to any server or cloud service.

2. **We do not sell your data.**
   SpendArc does not sell, share, or monetize user data in any form — not to advertisers, analytics companies, or any third party.

3. **We do not track you.**
   SpendArc does not include advertising SDKs, behavioral tracking, or analytics that collect personally identifiable information.

---

## What Data Does SpendArc Handle?

| Data Type | How It's Handled |
|-----------|-----------------|
| **Bank/UPI SMS** | If you grant SMS permission, SpendArc reads transaction-related messages to extract: amount, date, merchant name, and transaction type. **Raw SMS messages are not stored.** Only the parsed transaction metadata is saved locally on your device. |
| **Transactions** | Expenses, income, and transfers you enter manually or that are parsed from SMS. Stored locally on your device. |
| **Budgets & Goals** | Your budget limits and savings goals. Stored locally on your device. |
| **AI Conversations** | When you chat with the AI assistant, your messages are sent to a third-party AI service to generate responses. No conversation history is stored on external servers beyond the duration of the request. |
| **Preferences** | App settings such as completed tours and display preferences. Stored locally on your device. |

---

## What SpendArc Does NOT Do

- ❌ Does not store raw SMS messages
- ❌ Does not upload financial data to the cloud
- ❌ Does not share data with third parties (except the AI service for chat functionality)
- ❌ Does not log personally identifiable information
- ❌ Does not use advertising or behavioral tracking SDKs
- ❌ Does not enable cloud backups of sensitive data

---

## SMS Permission

- SMS access is **optional**. The app works fully without it.
- If granted, SpendArc reads only bank and UPI transaction messages.
- Promotional, OTP, and non-financial messages are filtered out and never processed.
- You can revoke SMS permission at any time through your device settings.

---

## AI Assistant

- The AI chat feature sends your questions to a third-party AI service to generate responses.
- Only the content of your current conversation is transmitted — no stored history, no account data beyond what you ask about in that session.
- The AI service does not retain your data after responding.

---

## Data Storage

- All financial data is stored **locally on your device** using an encrypted database.
- No cloud sync. No remote backups by the app.
- Uninstalling the app removes all stored data.
