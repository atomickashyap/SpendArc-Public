# ✨ Features

SpendArc is designed to make personal finance simple, private, and insightful.

---

## 📊 Expense & Income Tracking

- **Automatic transaction capture** — SpendArc can read your bank and UPI SMS messages to record transactions automatically (Android, with your permission).
- **Manual entry** — Add expenses, income, and transfers between accounts anytime.
- **Edit and delete** — Full control to correct or remove any transaction.
- **Category tagging** — Organize every transaction by category (food, transport, shopping, etc.).

---

## 🏦 Multiple Accounts

- Track balances across **bank accounts, cash, and digital wallets**.
- See a consolidated view of your finances in one place.
- Move money between accounts with transfer tracking.

---

## 📋 Budgets

- Set **monthly budgets** for individual spending categories.
- Visual progress bars show how much you've spent vs. your limit.
- Historical view to see how your spending compares month over month.

---

## 🎯 Savings Goals

- Define savings goals with a **target amount, timeline, and priority**.
- Track progress toward each goal.
- Get status updates: on track, at risk, or needs attention.

---

## 🤖 AI Assistant — "Ask SpendArc"

- Chat with SpendArc in **natural language** to ask about your spending.
- Get answers like *"How much did I spend on food this month?"* or *"Can I afford a ₹5,000 purchase?"*
- The assistant can help you **create goals** and **check if a purchase fits your budget**.
- Contextual follow-up — the conversation remembers what you asked earlier in the session.
- Suggested prompts help you get started.

---

## 🛡️ Safe-to-Spend

- Before making a purchase, check if it's **safe, risky, or not recommended** given your budget and goals.
- A quick way to make smarter spending decisions in the moment.

---

## 🧠 Smart Categorization

- SpendArc **learns from your choices** — when you categorize a transaction, it remembers for next time.
- Similar transactions are auto-categorized in the future.
- You always have full control to override any suggestion.

---

## 📈 Dashboard

- At-a-glance **monthly spending summary**.
- Visual breakdown by category.
- Recent transactions.
- Budget status cards.
- AI-generated insight cards with tips and alerts.

---

## 🎓 Guided Tours

- Interactive walkthroughs help you learn every feature when you first use it.
- Skip anytime — they won't come back once completed.

---

## 🔒 Privacy-First Design

- **All data stays on your device** — no cloud sync, no servers storing your finances.
- No raw SMS messages are stored — only the relevant transaction details are extracted.
- No personal information is logged.
- Works fully offline after setup.

---

## 📱 Works Without SMS Permission

SpendArc is fully functional even if you choose not to grant SMS access:
- Add expenses, income, and transfers manually.
- Use budgets, goals, and the AI assistant.
- SMS auto-tracking is optional — not required.

---

## 🎨 Polished Experience

- Clean, modern design with consistent styling.
- Subtle haptic feedback for a tactile, responsive feel.
- Smooth animations and transitions.
- Dark-themed interface elements for comfortable use.

---

> *Features described here reflect the current version of SpendArc and may evolve over time.*
