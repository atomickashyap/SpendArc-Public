# 🗺️ Roadmap

This is a high-level look at areas we're exploring for SpendArc's future.

> ⚠️ **Nothing here is a commitment or guarantee.** Items may change, be reprioritized, or be dropped entirely. No timelines are provided.

---

## 🟢 Planned

*Areas we intend to work on.*

- Recurring transaction support (subscriptions, rent, salary)
- Enhanced budget insights and notifications
- Improved onboarding experience

---

## 🟡 Considering

*Ideas we're actively evaluating.*

- Multi-currency support
- Export financial data (CSV / PDF reports)
- Widgets for quick balance and spending glance
- Family / shared expense tracking
- Bill reminders and due-date alerts

---

## 🔵 Exploring

*Early ideas — no concrete plans yet.*

- iOS availability
- Desktop companion app
- Custom categories with icons
- Smarter AI recommendations based on spending patterns
- Dark / light theme toggle
